Andrei Jay

Bio

Andrei Jay is a video artist, educator, tool maker, and community organizer. They are best known for developing open source digital video synthesis tools, their work organizing and leading workshops with Phase Space, and for their audio visual artwork.

For Hire

Andrei is available for work including

  • Live talks and guest lectures on video art, video feedback, creative coding, digital design, & fundamentals of designing synthesis systems
  • Workshops on video art as performance, either one offs, or the week long Video Art Boot Camp *add link here
  • Consulting and tech work for designing synthesis systems, live audio visual performances, and audio visual installations
